About Us

Myanmar Honey Food Factory Co.,LTD produce our main products, spring rolls, with experienced Taiwanese technique which has been well-known for more than 50 years. Every piece of Myanmar Honey Ready-made Fried Spring roll is fully handmade. In 2011, the production line relocated in East Dagon Industrial Zone, Yangon, Myanmar and started to penetrate the Myanmar market in 2013. Based on the rich resources of Myanmar, our ingredients are guaranteed to be fresh and healthy to produce quality food.
